BetBoom Team is a Russian Dota 2 esports team founded on April 18, 2022. The first roster included former Winstrike Team players: Nikita 'Daxak' Kuzmin, Denis 'Larl' Segitov, Evgeniy 'Noticed' Ignatenko, Maxim 'Forcemajor' Meretsky, Akbar 'SoNNeikO' Butaev and Andrey 'Mag' Chipenko in the coaching position. The first tournament where players performed under the BetBoom Team tag was Winline Dota 2 Champions League Season 9. The team received a direct invitation to the playoff stage, where they advanced through the upper bracket to the grand final, losing only on one map in a match against Virtus.pro.
