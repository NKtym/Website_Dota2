The International 2023

What's The International?

The International (TI) is an annual esports world championship for the five-on-five video game Dota 2. Produced by the game's developer Valve, the International is the final event of the Dota Pro Circuit (DPC) and consists of 20 teams: 12 based on final results from the DPC; six from North America, South America, Southeast Asia, China, Eastern Europe, and Western Europe regional leagues; and two from last-chance qualifiers.

Place

The annual main Dota 2 tournament, The International, will once again host Seattle in 2023, the city where developers Valve are headquartered. The tournament has previously been held in Seattle, Washington, six times in a row - from 2012 to 2017. After that, Valve decided to try new locations, and TI was held in a different region each time.
